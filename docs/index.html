About

Preparing Daily reports can be very stressful and time consuming for Welding Inspectors, especially in large fabrication projects. Most times, Inspectors would have to take work home, or work extra hours to prepare and send these reports. Having a tool that can help them prepare and key in the data needed at the point of collection can greatly reduce the stress and extra time needed to get these reports ready. With Kwikreport, we aim to give Inspectors an easy to use tool that can help them quickly create, edit and send their reports.
